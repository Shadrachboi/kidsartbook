Meet Anita Tafa

As a former elementary school art teacher and mother of two, I've seen firsthand how children's artwork captures their imagination, creativity, and development at each stage of childhood.

After years of helping parents figure out what to do with the mountains of artwork their children created, I founded Kids ArtBook in 2023 with a simple mission: to help families preserve their children's artistic journey in a beautiful, lasting way.

Each book we create is a labor of love, professionally designed to showcase your child's creativity while eliminating the guilt of discarding original pieces. Our team of designers treats each artwork with care and respect, ensuring the final product is something your family will treasure for generations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many artwork pieces can I include?

The number of artwork pieces depends on your chosen package. The Basic Book includes up to 15 pieces, the Deluxe Book includes up to 25 pieces, and the Ultimate Bundle includes up to 35 pieces. If you have more artwork you'd like to include, please contact us for custom options.

How do I submit the artwork?

After backing the project, you'll receive access to our secure upload portal where you can submit photos of your child's artwork. We recommend taking photos in natural daylight without flash, making sure the entire artwork is visible and in focus. Our team will professionally enhance and optimize each image.

How long does it take to receive my book?

Once you've submitted all your artwork, it typically takes 3-4 weeks for us to design, print, and ship your book. Before printing, we'll send you a digital proof for approval, which may add a few days to the process depending on how quickly you respond.

What is the quality of the book?

All our books feature premium quality materials including a durable hardcover binding, thick 100 lb glossy paper for vibrant color reproduction, and archival-quality printing that's designed to last. The books measure 8.5" x 11" and are professionally bound with a lay-flat design for easy viewing.

Can I include artwork from multiple children?

Yes! Many families choose to create books that include artwork from siblings. You can either create separate books for each child or combine their artwork into one family collection. Just make sure to clearly label which artwork belongs to which child during the submission process.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Free shipping is included for US orders. International shipping costs vary by location and will be calculated at checkout. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs fees, which are the responsibility of the recipient.
